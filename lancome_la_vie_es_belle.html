How to find your perfect shade:

Choose the intensity level of your skin: from light (1) to dark (6 and above). The letters C, N and W indicate the undertones of the skin:

-C = cool, for pink tones

-N = neutral, for a uniform pink and golden tone

-W = warm, for a golden tone
